Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a birthday?

Open the app, tap the "+" button, enter the person's name and birthday date, and save. The app will automatically remind you before their special day.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. All your birthday data is stored locally on your device. We never collect, access, or share your personal information. Check our Privacy Policy for more details.

Can I sync birthdays across devices?

Currently, the app stores data locally on your device. Cloud sync features may be added in future updates.

How do I delete a birthday?

Swipe left on any birthday entry in your list and tap the delete button.
