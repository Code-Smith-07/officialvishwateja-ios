Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026 · Developer: Vishwateja S B (Official Vishwateja)

1. Summary

Control Pro – Desktop Remote turns your iPhone or iPad into a wireless trackpad, keyboard, and live screen mirror for your own computer. It is designed so that your data never leaves your home or office network. We collect nothing.

2. What we collect

Nothing. Control Pro collects no personal information, no analytics, no usage data, and no identifiers. There are no accounts, no sign-ups, and no third-party SDKs.

3. How the app works with your data

  • The iOS app and the companion desktop host app talk directly to each other over your local network. Screen video, audio, keyboard input, pointer input, and optional clipboard text travel only between your own devices.
  • All connections are encrypted with TLS. Pairing requires a one-time code shown on your computer.
  • Nothing is sent to us or to any third party. We operate no servers, and the apps make no internet connections.
  • Optional clipboard sync is off by default and only shares clipboard text between your own devices while enabled.

4. Permissions we request

  • Local network (iOS and desktop host) — used solely to discover and connect to your own computer.
  • Screen recording (desktop host) — used solely to mirror your computer's screen to your iOS device.
  • Accessibility (desktop host) — used solely to move the mouse and type on your computer when you control it from your iOS device.

5. What we do not do

  • We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data.
  • We do not use analytics or tracking of any kind.
  • We do not show advertising in the app.
  • We do not operate any cloud servers — your screen and input never leave your network.

6. Children

Control Pro collects no data from anyone, including children.

7. Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be published at this address with a new "Last updated" date.

8. Contact

Questions? Email officialvishwateja@proton.me or open an issue on GitHub.

Bottom line: Everything stays on your local network, encrypted end to end. No accounts, no analytics, no cloud — we never see your data.
