Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an API key?

For online models (GPT, Claude, Gemini, OpenRouter, Groq, etc.), yes — bring your own key from the provider (BYOK). For on-device models on iPhone, no key is required after you download a model.

Where are my API keys stored?

On iOS they live in the Keychain. On the web companion they live in your browser’s localStorage. They are never sold and are not used by us to call models on your behalf without your configuration.

Can I use Chat Blues offline?

Yes. Open Settings → On-Device AI, download a model (Wi‑Fi recommended), load it, and chat without sending prompts to the cloud.

How do Sign in with Apple / Google work?

Sign-in is optional. It enables chat history sync across devices via Firebase. You can use the app without an account for local and on-device features.

How do I delete my account?

Email officialvishwateja@proton.me from the address associated with your account. We wipe your Firestore data (and related Storage objects) within 30 days. You can also delete individual chats inside the app anytime.

Why does the app ask for microphone / camera / photos?

Microphone and speech recognition are for voice input. Camera and photo library are only used when you choose to attach or save images. Local network is only for optional self-hosted AI (e.g. Ollama on your LAN).

What iOS version is required?

Chat Blues targets iOS 16.0 and later on iPhone.
